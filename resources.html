Resources

Epicenter Lens provides vital information to help communities and beyond prepare for and respond to disasters. Our resources span emergency hotlines, safety support, and community preparedness programs, guiding you to stay safe and resilient.

Emergency Contacts

National Emergency Helpline: Dial 112 for immediate assistance in life-threatening situations.
Disaster Management Helpline: The National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) can be reached at 1078 for disaster-related inquiries and assistance.
Indian Red Cross Society: For shelter, first aid, and disaster relief, reach out at +91-11-23716441 or visit Indian Red Cross.

Safety Support

Kiran safety Helpline: Dial 1800-599-0019 for 24/7 support, providing safety counseling and resources.
iCALL - Tata Institute of Social Sciences: Available at +91-9152987821, iCALL offers free counseling services through phone, chat, and email.
Online Therapy Directory: Find a certified therapist via Therapize India.

Preparedness & Community Programs

NDMA Community Preparedness: Access guides and checklists for disaster preparedness from the National Disaster Management Authority.
NDRF Training Programs: The National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) offers community training programs on emergency response skills and disaster management.
Local Support Networks: Connect with local support groups to share experiences, learn best practices, and build community resilience.
